About Smart Money Smarts

Empowering students with money skills — short, friendly, and Canada-first.

Our Mission

Smart Money Smarts was started by students and young professionals who remember juggling part-time work, loans, and ramen-budget living. Most finance advice is full of jargon — we cut the fluff and speak your language. We write practical guides on chequing accounts, budgets, saving hacks, and small investing using $CDN examples.

Meet the founder

Hi — I’m Andreas, a student and content creator based in Canada. I love plain-English finance, spreadsheets that actually get used, and tips that make student life easier. Our team is small but rooted in real student experience and backed by freelance finance reviewers.
